▼
"Call out any malfunction or warning. When any malfunction occurs before V1, I will call 'STOP' or 'GO'. Before V1, I will reject the takeoff for engine failure, any warning, caution or condition that significantly affects the safety of the flight."

AIRCRAFT DEFECT — DECISION FLOW
Before & after pushback
▼
Before Pushback:
1. Deferred in DDL? → Yes: MEL repetitive task? No → No further action | Yes → Engineer 'M' procedure, ATL entry
2. FCOM/eQRH reset available? → Try reset. Successful → 'FOR INFO' ATL, no further action
3. In SIN? → Yes: MAINTROL – engineer to rectify/defer. ATL & DDL entries required
4. Not in SIN → Contact local engineers. If unavailable → MAINTROL
Commanders may defer MEL items with 'O' or NO procedures in consultation with MAINTROL.
After Pushback to T/O Roll:
1. FCOM/eQRH reset? → Try. Successful → ATL 'FOR INFO', continue
2. Refer to MEL → 'NO DISPATCH'? → Return to bay, contact engineers, ATL entry
3. 'M' procedure? → Return to bay | 'O' procedure? → Carry out, consult MAINTROL, continue
